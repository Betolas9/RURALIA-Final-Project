Where rural tourism meets modernity.

We are redefining how you experience the Portuguese countryside by combining advanced technology with the authenticity of rural landscapes.

With our focus on gamification, every step becomes an adventure, encouraging you to explore, learn, and connect with local traditions.

Get ready for an exciting journey where intuitive tracking and a community of passionate travelers will accompany you in a revolution of rural tourism in Portugal.

Ruralia is designed to be your ideal travel companion, always at hand.

With the growing reliance on smartphones, we recognize the importance of providing an optimized mobile experience. By using Ruralia on your mobile device, you'll have the freedom to conveniently and immersively explore the Portuguese countryside anytime, anywhere.

Unleash your curiosity and embrace effortless mobility while experiencing authentic rural tourism in Portugal.

At Ruralia, we strongly believe in promoting a positive social impact. In addition to offering unforgettable rural tourism experiences to our users, we are invested in the preservation and valorization of local communities. We collaborate with farmers, artisans, and rural entrepreneurs, providing a platform for them to share their stories, products, and traditions.

Together, we strengthen the cultural and economic roots of rural communities, ensuring that their stories continue to be told and valued.
